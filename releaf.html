RELEAF

Grow Your Mental Health, One Day at a Time - Nurture Your Mind Like a Bonsai

Problem Statement

In today’s fast-paced world, people are constantly juggling responsibilities while struggling to find time for their mental health. Existing wellness apps often feel transactional, overwhelming users with tasks and reminders rather than fostering meaningful emotional growth. This results in burnout and disconnection, leaving users feeling like they’re failing their well-being journey. Many people crave a deeper, more organic approach to self-care, one that allows them to reflect, breathe, and grow at their own pace.

Solution

Releaf simplifies mental wellness through a unique, calming experience—much like caring for a bonsai. By checking in daily with their mood and nurturing a digital tree, users are gently reminded to take small, meaningful steps towards their mental health. Releaf offers tools like journaling, breathing exercises, and letters to your future self—designed to reflect growth over time, without pressure or overwhelming features.

The Design Process

The Research

How it all started...

Releaf began as a concept inspired by a dream—focusing on the mental well-being of users through symbolic, nurturing elements. The vision was to create a mental health app that mirrored growth and care, much like tending to a bonsai tree. This simple, symbolic approach was a response to how many existing apps feel impersonal or task-heavy, which often leads to burnout or disinterest.

What was found?

Research shows that mental health apps often struggle with retention rates, primarily because they focus on tasks rather than personal reflection. Users abandon these apps when they feel pressured to complete rigid routines, which often leads to feelings of failure instead of progress.
Studies suggest that more symbolic approaches—like Releaf's digital bonsai—encourage higher engagement, as users see their emotional growth mirrored in visual, interactive representations​.

What can change?

Releaf introduces an innovative, personalized approach to mental health. The app moves away from rigid daily tasks and embraces a more flexible, organic interaction, offering users the chance to grow their bonsai in a natural way. With features like mood tracking, journaling, and meditation, users nurture both their minds and their digital bonsai over time.
