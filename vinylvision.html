VINYLVISION

When Music meets Augmented reality

Problem Statement

Vinyl collectors and music fans are looking for a way to connect their love for physical albums with modern technology. At the same time, libraries and record shops struggle with engaging customers and reducing album returns from impulse buys, making it hard to stand out in an increasingly digital world.

Solution

Vinyl Vision bridges this gap by offering an augmented reality (AR) experience where users can scan album covers in-store to unlock interactive content, like artist stories and track previews. This not only enhances the user experience but also helps retailers offer a tech-forward, engaging solution that adds value and reduces returns.
