Sounds and sails

Personalized music journeys. Discover the perfect soundtrack for every moment, guided by the winds of AI.

Problem Statement

In a world overflowing with playlists and recommendations, listeners often feel disconnected from the music that speaks to their unique emotions. Generic algorithms miss the deeper connection, leaving people navigating through endless songs that don't resonate with their moods.

Solution

Sounds and Sails takes the guesswork out of finding music that fits your mood. With just a word or feeling, the app uses AI to find songs and generate playlists that truly resonate with your emotions—no endless scrolling, just the right vibe when you need it.

The Design Process

How does the ai work here?

Sounds and Sails begins by offering a curated database of songs tied to specific emotions or keywords. Users can type in a feeling—like 'calm' or 'nostalgic'—and the app instantly generates songs for a playlist that fits that mood. Users can add these songs to their own playlist, which they can update and refine over time.

Additionally, users can suggest new songs that resonate with certain emotions, contributing to the app's evolving database. Powered by AI, the app learns from user preferences and feedback, continuously improving its ability to deliver more personalized and emotionally connected playlists. As more people engage, the AI refines its recommendations, offering an ever-growing library of music that resonates on a deeper level.

The Research

How it all started...

The research began after users expressed frustration with existing music platforms, especially how personalized playlists are often disrupted by irrelevant or overly commercialized songs. For example, many playlists labeled "calm" or "focus" on platforms like Spotify end up including mainstream hits like Billie Eilish's "Wildflower" or "Espresso" that don’t fit the intended vibe.
This mismatch disrupts the user's emotional connection to the playlist, leading to dissatisfaction and reduced engagement.

What was found?

Through observation and interviews, it became clear that users are seeking emotionally cohesive playlists that can capture their specific moods or experiences. Current algorithms struggle to fully understand these nuanced emotional requests, leading to generic or repetitive recommendations. In a survey conducted across different platforms, many users reported wanting a system that felt more personalized to their feelings, not just general categories like “happy” or “sad.”

What can change?

By integrating AI that learns from community feedback and user suggestions, Sounds and Sails can evolve its music recommendations.
The key is allowing users to submit songs they feel fit particular emotions and moods, while the AI continues to adapt and refine its playlist-building process. This user-driven model makes the app more emotionally intelligent over time, improving song relevance and creating a more immersive and accurate musical journey.
